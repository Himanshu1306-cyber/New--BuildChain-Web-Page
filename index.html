About BuildChain

BuildChain is a next-generation construction management portal that leverages Web3 Technology to bring transparency to the supply chain. We believe that every brick and bag of cement used in a project should be accounted for in a public, tamper-proof ledger.

Our platform connects Contractors and Project Admins in a seamless environment where data is verified and stored forever on the blockchain.
